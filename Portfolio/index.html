Reddit Sentiment Analysis Using AWS Services

Publisher: Springer   |   Date: March 11, 2025

Our study explores sentiment analysis of Reddit comments using AWS cloud services. We built a data pipeline with PRAW, Kinesis Firehose, AWS Glue, and Athena to analyze sentiment patterns. AWS Comprehend achieved an F1 score of 0.83 and 82.5% accuracy, showcasing its effectiveness.

Show Publication

Automatic Number Plate Recognition: A Deep Dive into YOLOv8 and ResNet-50 Integration

Publisher: IEEE   |   Date: April 18, 2024

Our research tackles the complexities of identifying car number plates in India, where standards are often not followed. Our proposed model employs YOLOv8 for object detection and ResNet-50 for feature extraction, ensuring robust performance under varied conditions. Achieving a 98.6% success rate in localizing single-line number plates and a 97.81% success rate in character recognition, this study enhances the accuracy and reliability of ANPR systems.
